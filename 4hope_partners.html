Our Partners

4Hope Kenya is powered by partnerships across NYC and Nairobi. Every partner plays a real role in building something that lasts.

Community and Institutional Partners

Who We Build With

🏠
Community Partner — Kenya
Children's Garden Home
📍 Nairobi, Kenya
Founded in 2001 by Moses and Sylvia Ndungu. Children's Garden Home has cared for over 2,000 orphaned and vulnerable children. Today nearly 250 children are in care. 4Hope serves their students directly — delivering STEM education and building bikes designed for their community.
🌍 Primary Beneficiary Partner
🤖
Teaching Partner — NYC
Stuy Fusion FTC Team 479
📍 Stuyvesant High School, NYC
Award-winning FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team from Stuyvesant High School. Stuy Fusion delivers CAD and coding lessons directly to our Kenya students covering Unit 5 Mechanics. They also provide engineering mentorship through their professional mentor network.
🔧 CAD and Coding Teaching Partner
🏛️
Community Partner — NYC
Queens Community House
📍 Jackson Heights, Queens, NYC
Queens Community House provides community support across Jackson Heights and beyond. 4Hope partners with QCH to deliver tech literacy education to LGBTQ+ seniors and underserved community members — bringing the same mission of accessible education from Kenya to Queens.
🌆 NYC Community Outreach Partner
⚙️
Engineering Partner
Engineers Without Borders USA
📍 USA — Remote
EWB-USA connects 4Hope with professional mechanical engineers who review our bike designs and provide technical guidance. Their expertise ensures our $200 mountain bike is not just affordable but safe, durable and genuinely built for Kenyan terrain.
🔩 Engineering Mentor Partner
