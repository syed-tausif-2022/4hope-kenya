💻 Tech for Everyone

Tech Seniors Program

Bringing digital literacy and tech assistance to seniors and newcomers in Queens, NYC — because everyone deserves access to technology.

Who We Help

Technology for Everyone

👴
Senior Community Members
Helping seniors navigate smartphones, video calls and the internet so they can stay connected with family.
🌍
Newcomers and Immigrants
Supporting newcomers to NYC with essential digital tools — email, maps, translation apps and online safety.
🤝
Anyone Who Needs Help
No question is too small. Whether it is a frozen screen or setting up email — we help at your pace.


Our Teaching Approach

We go at your pace. No question is too small. We celebrate every small win. Large text handouts available on request. Sessions held at a comfortable pace for all ability levels. Ideal group size 5-10 seniors for personalized attention.
