Our Partners

4Hope Kenya is powered by partnerships across NYC and Nairobi. Every partner plays a real role in building something that lasts.

Community and Institutional Partners

Who We Build With

🏠
Community Partner — Kenya
Children's Garden Home
📍 Nairobi, Kenya
Founded in 2001 by Moses and Sylvia Ndungu. Cared for over 2,000 orphaned children. Today nearly 250 children are in care. 4Hope serves their students directly — delivering STEM education and building bikes designed for their community.
🌍 Primary Beneficiary Partner
🤖
Teaching Partner — NYC
Stuy Fusion FTC Team 479
📍 Stuyvesant High School, NYC
Award-winning FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team. Stuy Fusion delivers CAD and coding lessons to our Kenya students for Unit 5 Mechanics. They also provide engineering mentorship through their professional mentor network.
🔧 CAD and Coding Teaching Partner
🏛️
Community Partner — NYC
Queens Community House
📍 Jackson Heights, Queens, NYC
Queens Community House provides community support across Jackson Heights. 4Hope partners with QCH to deliver tech literacy education to seniors and underserved community members. Contact: Alma Reyes — 347-785-8888.
🌆 NYC Community Outreach Partner
💜
Community Partner — NYC
Creando Algo Especial
📍 New York City — Est. 2016
A nonprofit organization supporting people with disabilities and the neurodivergent community. 4Hope works alongside Creando Algo Especial in serving Queens community members through inclusive tech education and outreach.
♿ Inclusive Community Partner
⚙️
Engineering Partner
Engineers Without Borders USA
📍 USA — Remote
EWB-USA connects 4Hope with professional mechanical engineers who review our bike designs and provide technical guidance. Their expertise ensures our $197 mountain bike is safe, durable and genuinely built for Kenyan terrain.
🔩 Engineering Mentor Partner
📚
Venue Partner — NYC
Poetshouse Library
📍 NYC
Poetshouse is where Syed works and builds community. As Technical Operations and Curator volunteer, 4Hope has used Poetshouse as a base for community outreach and poetry events connecting the arts with the mission.
📖 Community Venue Partner
